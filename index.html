Powerpuff Girls

Who is the youngest Powerpuff Girl?

Accidentally created in a lab by Professor Utonium, superpowered sisters Blossom, Bubbles and Buttercup regularly save Townsville from evildoers such as Mojo Jojo, the Gangreen Gang and Him. Blossom is the self-proclaimed leader of the trio, Bubbles is the cute one and Buttercup is the toughest one.

They each had a designated Powerpuff Girl. The oldest was Blossom, the four-year-old was Buttercup, and the youngest was always Bubbles.

Fun Facts About The Powerpuff Girls:

  1. They were originally known as ‘the whoop-ass girls.’
  2. The girls’ eyes were inspired by margaret keane’s work.
  3. Buttercup’s name was initially bud.
  4. They had different names in different places.
  5. From the beginning, the show was very, very popular.
